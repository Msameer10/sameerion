"SAMEERION"..?

Well it's an infusion of three words;  my name SAMEER,

RI - derived from arabic word رياح "riah" meaning wind, because, one the meanings of my name is 'Wind' and

ION - meaning "moving" in Greek, symbolizes speed through rapid motion and energy transfer. It conveys achievement as ions drive essential processes, reflecting progress and transformation, it also suggests a domain by representing centrality and influence in systems.
